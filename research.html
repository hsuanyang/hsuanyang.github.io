Research-based artworks:
Nation, Identity and Governmentality

My research Based on “the Governmentalities of an Airport: Heterotopia and Confession” (Salter M.B., 2007) and Michel Foucault’s study of discipline and sociology, especially thinking about how the nation-state generates Other, create the racial difference, and raises boundaries that delimit and support the modern subject of sovereignty. My projects focused on the international politics and the political power in everyday life, thinking about how ‘nation’ and ‘identity’ work in the sight of border, and the complex social psychology under these issues.
